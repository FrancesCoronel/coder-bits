Adonit Jot Script Fine Point Stylus for iOS

Evernote Edition

1.9mm Tip - GRAY


Introducing the Adonit Jot Script Evernote Edition Fine Point Stylus for iOS devices! Boasting a 1.9mm tip and made of stainless steel, aluminum, and plastic, the Jot Script Stylus is weighted such that it feels like a top-of-the-line ballpoint pen, while also writing and flowing like a regular pen.

With Pixelpoint technology, combining the Bluetooth LE, accelerometer, and Adonit's SDK, the Jot Script Stylus features superior precision for the stylus' tip, giving you better control on the screen and more accuracy for each line and letter you write.

The Jot Script Stylus employs standard AAA lithium batteries and connects by Bluetooth 4.0. The Jot Script is designed to work smoothly with Penultimate and other popular apps.

The Jot Script Stylus can be used with all iOS devices. Bluetooth 4.0 is required for advanced features, such as palm rejection (Bluetooth 4.0 available on iPad 3rd generation and newer, iPad mini, iPhone 4S and newer).
